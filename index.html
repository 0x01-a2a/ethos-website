Privacy

Your data is
yours. Fully.

Most apps that claim to be private still collect anonymized telemetry, sync to a cloud, or require an account. We don't. The architecture makes it impossible.

  • All AI inference runs on-device via Apple MLX
  • No account, no sign-in, no email required
  • SQLite database lives only in your app sandbox
  • No analytics SDK, no crash reporting cloud
  • Deleting the app deletes everything
